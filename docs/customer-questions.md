# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title
Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform

## Target Respondent
University students at RSU who have freelance experience or are interested in finding freelance work. Also small business owners or startup clients who have hired or want to hire student talent.

## Consent and Context
May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?
Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

---

## Section 1: Recent Experience

1. Tell us about the last time you tried to find a freelance job or project as a student. What happened from start to finish?

2. What were you trying to accomplish at that time? For example, were you looking for income, experience, or portfolio work?

3. How did that experience make you feel? What went well and what did not?

---

## Section 2: Current Workaround

4. How do you currently find freelance work or clients as a student? What tools, apps, platforms, or methods do you use?

5. What is difficult, slow, confusing, or frustrating about the platforms or methods you currently use?

6. Have you ever given up looking for freelance work because the process was too difficult or competitive? What happened?

---

## Section 3: Pain Severity and Consequence

7. How often do you face difficulty finding suitable freelance opportunities as a student? Is it every week, every month, or occasionally?

8. On a scale of 1 to 5, how serious is this problem for you personally? Can you explain why you chose that number?

9. What happens if this problem is not solved? How does it affect your studies, income, or career development?

---

## Section 4: Platform and Trust

10. If a platform existed specifically for university students to offer freelance services, what features would matter most to you? What would make you trust it?

---

## Closing

11. Is there anything else we should understand about the challenges students face when looking for freelance work?

12. May we use your anonymized response as part of our ICT105 course project evidence?
