---

## 2. Evidence Supporting the Decision

The MVP validation results demonstrate that the Student Internship & Entry-Level Job Platform successfully addresses its primary objective of connecting university students with verified employers offering internship and entry-level job opportunities.

Key validation evidence includes:

- **50 participants** completed the validation experiment.
- **45 participants successfully completed** their assigned tasks, resulting in a **90% task completion rate**.
- The prototype achieved an **average Ease of Use score of 4.3/5**.
- The **average Interest Level was 4.4/5**, indicating that most participants would consider using the platform if it were available.
- Most usability issues were minor and related to the verification workflow, dashboard navigation, and job search filters rather than the overall concept of the platform.

These findings indicate that the MVP direction is valid and should continue with usability improvements before the final implementation.

## 4. Requirements to Improve

| Requirement ID | Problem Found | Improvement Needed |
|---|---|---|
| FR-05 | Some participants were unsure about verification progress. | Improve verification status indicators and provide clearer feedback messages. |
| FR-11 | Users requested more flexible filtering options when searching for jobs. | Add additional search filters and improve filter visibility. |

---

## 5. Prototype Changes Before Next Lab

The team will make the following improvements before continuing development:

- Improve the verification workflow by providing clearer status updates and notifications.
- Enhance job search with additional filtering and sorting options.
- Simplify the Student Dashboard layout to improve readability.
- Improve navigation consistency across all pages.
- Refine interface labels and user guidance based on participant feedback.
- Fix minor usability issues identified during customer validation.
