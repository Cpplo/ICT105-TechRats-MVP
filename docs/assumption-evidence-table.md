# Assumption vs Evidence Table

## Project Title
Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform

## Purpose
This table separates what the team believes (assumptions) from what users actually say or do (evidence). Evidence is collected from 8 customer discovery responses (R001–R008) conducted in Lab 03.

---

## Assumption vs Evidence

| # | Team Assumption | Evidence Found | Source | Validated? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Students struggle to find freelance work on general platforms like Fiverr and Upwork | R001 felt invisible on Facebook groups; R003 gave up at Fiverr profile creation; R005 had Upwork account restricted | R001, R003, R005 | Validated |
| 2 | Students want a dedicated freelance platform for university students | R001 requested a student-only space with RSU badge; R003 wanted beginner-friendly onboarding; R005 said a local platform would solve restrictions | R001, R003, R005 | Validated |
| 3 | Clients prefer hiring students for affordability | R006 confirmed wanting affordable creative help; R007 posted for low-cost UI mockup | R006, R007 | Validated |
| 4 | Portfolio and academic profile increases client trust | R006 could not verify student skills; R007 hired wrong person due to no structured profile; R008 felt work was unrecognized without formal proof | R006, R007, R008 | Validated |
| 5 | Students are willing to use a new platform if it is student-focused | R001, R003, R005 all expressed strong interest in a student-specific platform with relevant features | R001, R003, R005 | Validated |
| 6 | The problem happens frequently enough to justify a solution | R005 faces difficulty weekly; R001 monthly; average severity across 8 responses is 3.75/5 | R001–R008 | Validated |
| 7 | Students currently use informal methods like LINE groups and personal networks | R002 uses LINE and personal network; R004 uses Instagram and word-of-mouth; R007 posted on LINE group | R002, R004, R007 | Validated |
| 8 | Payment protection is a concern for student freelancers | R004 experienced late payment with no contract; R005 lost income due to account restriction; R002 worries about recurring payment | R002, R004, R005 | Validated |

## Key Insights
- High entry barrier for first-time student freelancers (R003, severity 5/5).
- Platform restrictions specifically affecting Thai students (R005, severity 5/5).
- Clients need structured profiles and portfolio verification to hire with confidence (R006, R007).
- Payment protection and contract templates are critical features for student trust (R002, R004).
