# Lab 04 - MVP Feature List

## MVP Decision Rule

The MVP focuses on solving the validated problem identified during customer discovery by helping university students and fresh graduates discover trustworthy internship and entry-level job opportunities while enabling employers to recruit verified beginner talent through a secure and transparent platform.

# Must-Have Features

These features represent the minimum functionality required to validate the Student Internship & Entry-Level Job Platform.

- User Registration & Login
- Student Profile Management
- Resume & Portfolio Management
- Employer Registration & Company Profile
- Verification Management
- Internship & Job Posting
- Browse & Search Jobs
- Beginner-Friendly Job Filtering
- Verified Company Profile Viewing
- Job Application Management
- Saved Jobs
- Student Dashboard
- Employer Dashboard
- Applicant Management

---

# Should-Have Features

These features improve platform quality, trust, and administration.

- Job Reporting & Moderation
- Admin Dashboard & Platform Management

---

# Could-Have Features

These features provide additional value but are outside the MVP scope.

- AI Job Recommendation
- Skill-Based Job Matching
- Resume Improvement Suggestions
- Email Notifications
- Calendar Integration

---

# Not Included in the MVP

The following features will not be implemented during the semester:

- Mobile Application
- Video Interview Scheduling
- Online Assessment System
- AI Resume Evaluation
- AI Interview Preparation
- Payroll or Salary Management
- Third-Party Authentication (Google/Microsoft Login)
- Real-Time Chat System
- Online Payment Integration

---

# Final Prototype Scope

## Included in MVP

The first version of the Student Internship & Entry-Level Job Platform will include:

- User Registration & Login
- Student Profile Management
- Resume & Portfolio Management
- Employer Registration & Company Profile
- Student and Employer Verification
- Internship & Job Posting
- Browse & Search Jobs
- Beginner-Friendly Job Filtering
- Verified Company Profile Viewing
- Job Application Management
- Saved Jobs
- Student Dashboard
- Employer Dashboard
- Applicant Management
- Job Reporting & Moderation
- Admin Dashboard & Platform Management

---

## Excluded from MVP

The following features are planned for future versions after MVP validation:

- AI Job Recommendation
- Skill-Based Job Matching
- Resume Improvement Suggestions
- Mobile Application
- Video Interview Scheduling
- Real-Time Messaging
- Push Notifications
- Calendar Integration
- Online Assessment System
- Third-Party Authentication
- AI Interview Preparation
- Analytics & Business Intelligence Enhancements

These features require additional development effort and are reserved for future versions after the MVP has been validated.
