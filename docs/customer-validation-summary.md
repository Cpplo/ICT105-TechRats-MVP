# Lab 08 - Customer Validation Summary

## 1. Project Title

**Student Internship & Entry-Level Job Platform**

---

The team conducted customer validation using Version 1.0 of the interactive MVP prototype. The prototype demonstrated the complete workflow for students, employers, and administrators, including registration, verification, job browsing, job applications, dashboard management, and verification approval.

- **Prototype Version:** v1.0 Interactive MVP Prototype

  - Student Registration and Login
  - Student Profile Completion
  - Student Verification Submission
  - Job Browsing and Search
  - Company Profile Viewing
  - Job Application
  - Employer Registration
  - Company Verification
  - Job Posting Management
  - Admin Verification Management
- **Related Requirements:** FR-01 – FR-30

| Test User ID | User Role | Why this user is relevant |

## 4. Validation Method

The MVP prototype was evaluated using a task-based usability testing approach. Participants were asked to complete realistic tasks based on their assigned roles while their interactions and feedback were recorded.

- **Testing Method:** Task-based usability testing with observation and feedback questionnaire
- **Date/Time:** 5–11 July 2026
- **Location / Platform:** Computer Laboratory and Online Prototype Testing
- **Number of Testers:** 50
- **Data Collected:**
  - Task completion status
  - Task completion time
  - Ease of Use rating
  - Usefulness rating
  - Interest Level
  - Confusion points
  - Most useful feature
  - Open feedback comments

---

## 5. Summary of Results

| Metric | Result | Interpretation |
|---|---:|---|
| Total test users | 50 | A sufficient number of participants representing all target user roles completed the validation. |
| Task success rate | 90% | Most participants successfully completed their assigned tasks without assistance. |
| Average feedback score | 4.3 / 5.0 | Participants found the prototype easy to use and understand. |
| Average interest level | 4.4 / 5.0 | Most participants expressed interest in using the platform if it were available. |
| Most common confusion point | Verification Process | Some users requested clearer verification progress and status updates. |

---

## 6. Key User Comments

Several common themes emerged during customer validation:

- Registration and login were straightforward and easy to complete.
- Job search functionality helped participants quickly locate suitable opportunities.
- Verification badges increased trust in both students and employers.
- Dashboard layouts were generally easy to understand.
- Participants requested clearer verification status messages and progress indicators.
- Some users suggested additional search filters and improved dashboard navigation.
- Overall, participants believed the platform would help students discover trustworthy internship and entry-level job opportunities.

---

## 7. Affected Requirements

| Requirement ID | Evidence Found | Required Prototype Improvement |
|---|---|---|
| FR-05 | Participants wanted clearer verification progress and status updates. | Improve verification workflow and status notifications. |
| FR-11 | Users requested more advanced search and filtering options. | Add additional search filters and improve search usability. |
| FR-17 | Some participants found dashboard navigation slightly confusing. | Improve dashboard layout, navigation, and information hierarchy. |

---

## 8. Conclusion

The customer validation results indicate that the MVP is **validated with minor revisions required**.

Participants successfully completed the core workflows, including registration, verification, job browsing, company profile viewing, job application, and dashboard navigation. Overall usability and interest ratings were high, demonstrating that the platform effectively addresses the identified problem of connecting university students with verified employers.

The identified issues are primarily related to usability improvements rather than fundamental design problems. Therefore, the project will continue with minor revisions before proceeding to the final implementation and presentation.
