# Experiment Script

## 1. Opening Script

Thank you for participating in this MVP usability experiment.

We are testing the **Student Internship & Entry-Level Job Platform**, which is designed to help university students find trustworthy internship and entry-level job opportunities while enabling verified employers to recruit qualified student talent.

During this session, please complete the assigned tasks naturally as if you were using the platform for the first time. Feel free to think aloud by explaining what you expect, what you are looking for, and if anything is confusing.

This experiment evaluates the platform, not your performance. There are no right or wrong answers. Your feedback will help improve the final prototype.

---

## 2. Test Scenario

Imagine you are a Rangsit University (RSU) student looking for an internship or entry-level job.

You recently discovered this platform and want to create an account, complete your profile, verify your identity, browse available opportunities, and apply for a suitable position. Along the way, you will evaluate whether the platform is easy to use and whether it provides enough information to help you trust employers before applying.

---

## 3. Tasks for Tester

| Task | Task Description | Expected Result |
|------|------------------|-----------------|
| T1 | Open the homepage and identify the purpose of the platform. | Tester understands that the platform connects verified students with verified employers for internships and entry-level jobs. |
| T2 | Register a new Student account and log in. | Student account is successfully created and login is completed. |
| T3 | Complete the Student Profile by adding personal information, education, skills, and portfolio. | Profile information is completed successfully. |
| T4 | Upload student verification documents. | Verification request is submitted and account status becomes **Pending Verification**. |
| T5 | Browse internship and entry-level job listings using search and filtering features. | Tester can easily find suitable job opportunities. |
| T6 | Open a company profile and check whether the company is verified. | Tester recognizes verification badges and understands company information before applying. |
| T7 | Attempt to apply for a job. | Verified users can apply successfully. Pending users understand why verification is required before applying. |
| T8 | Open the Student Dashboard and review application status, profile completion, and notifications. | Tester can easily understand dashboard information and current account status. |

---

## 4. Observation Notes

During the experiment, observe and record:

- Whether the tester completes each task successfully.
- Time required to complete each task.
- Navigation difficulties.
- Confusing interface elements.
- Pages where the tester hesitates.
- Any unexpected actions or mistakes.
- Questions asked by the tester.
- Suggestions for improving usability or functionality.

---

## 5. Closing Questions

1. Was the purpose of the platform clear when you first opened the homepage?
2. Was the registration process easy to complete?
3. Was the profile completion process straightforward?
4. Did you understand the student verification process?
5. Was it easy to browse and search for internship opportunities?
6. Did the company verification badge increase your confidence in employers?
7. Was the job application process easy to understand?
8. Was the Student Dashboard useful and easy to navigate?
9. Which feature did you find most useful?
10. What improvements would you recommend before the final prototype?
