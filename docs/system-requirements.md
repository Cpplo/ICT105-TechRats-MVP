# System Requirements

## Minimum Final Prototype Functionalities

These requirements define the minimum prototype functionalities for the Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform. The requirements are derived from the customer discovery findings, user persona, user stories, and MVP feature list. They describe the essential features required for the first functional prototype.

| Req ID | Minimum Prototype Functionality | What Students Must Show in Final Prototype |
| ------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **FR-01** | **Student Registration & Login** | Students shall be able to register and log in securely using their university email account. |
| **FR-02** | **Company Registration & Login** | Companies shall be able to register and log in securely using their business email and company account. |
| **FR-03** | **Student Profile Management** | Students shall be able to create, edit, and manage their profile including education, skills, experience, and contact information. |
| **FR-04** | **Portfolio Upload & Management** | Students shall be able to upload, edit, and manage portfolios containing previous work, certificates, achievements, and project samples. |
| **FR-05** | **Company Profile Management** | Companies shall be able to create, edit, and manage their company profile including logo, description, industry, location, website, and contact information. |
| **FR-06** | **Company Verification Request** | Companies shall be able to submit their business information for verification. The prototype shall display verification statuses such as Verified, Pending Verification, or Not Verified. |
| **FR-07** | **Project Posting & Management** | Companies shall be able to create, edit, update, and remove freelance project listings including title, description, required skills, budget, deadline, and category. |
| **FR-08** | **Browse & Search Freelance Projects** | Students shall be able to browse available projects and search or filter them by category, company, required skills, project type, or budget. |
| **FR-09** | **Company Profile Viewing** | Students shall be able to view company profiles including verification badge, company description, ratings, reviews, and project history before applying. |
| **FR-10** | **Project Application** | Students shall be able to apply for freelance projects by submitting their profile and portfolio to the selected company. |
| **FR-11** | **Application Status Tracking** | Students shall be able to monitor application progress such as Applied, Under Review, Accepted, Rejected, or Completed. Companies shall be able to update these statuses. |
| **FR-12** | **Student Portfolio Viewing** | Companies shall be able to browse student profiles and portfolios before selecting suitable applicants. |
| **FR-13** | **Messaging System** | Students and companies shall be able to exchange messages regarding project details before and during collaboration. |
| **FR-14** | **Company Ratings & Reviews** | Students shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for companies after project completion to improve transparency and trust. |
| **FR-15** | **Student Ratings & Reviews** | Companies shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for students after project completion to build professional credibility. |
| **FR-16** | **Input Validation & Confirmation Messages** | The system shall validate required fields, prevent incomplete submissions, and display confirmation or error messages after user actions such as registration, project posting, or application submission. |

---

## Minimum Technical Requirement

The final prototype must not be only a static presentation. It must be an interactive prototype where users can register, manage profiles, post or browse freelance projects, apply for projects, communicate through messaging, track application status, and submit reviews.

Acceptable platforms may include:

| Platform Type | Acceptable Prototype Form |
| ------------- | ------------------------- |
| Web app | HTML/CSS/JavaScript, React.js, Spring Boot, Node.js, or similar |
| Mobile app prototype | Figma clickable prototype, Flutter, React Native, or similar |
| Database | PostgreSQL, MySQL, Firebase, or similar |
| Authentication | University email authentication for students and secure company account authentication |
| Data-driven prototype | Database-backed prototype containing student accounts, company accounts, company profiles, project listings, applications, messaging, reviews, and verification status |
| UI/UX prototype | Figma prototype demonstrating realistic workflows for both students and companies, from registration to project completion |
