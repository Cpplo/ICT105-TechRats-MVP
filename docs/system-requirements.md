# System Requirements

## Minimum Final Prototype Functionalities

These requirements define the minimum prototype functionalities for the Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform. The requirements are derived from the customer discovery findings, user persona, user stories, and MVP feature list. They describe the essential features required for the first functional prototype.

| Req ID | Minimum Prototype Functionality | What Students Must Show in Final Prototype |
| ------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **FR-01** | **User Registration & Login** | Students and companies shall be able to register and log in securely using their respective accounts. Students register using their university email, while companies register using their business information. |
| **FR-02** | **Student Profile Management** | Students shall be able to create, edit, and manage their profile, including personal information, education, skills, experience, and contact details. |
| **FR-03** | **Portfolio Upload & Management** | Students shall be able to upload, edit, and manage portfolios containing previous work, projects, certificates, and achievements. |
| **FR-04** | **Company Profile Management** | Companies shall be able to create, edit, and manage their company profile, including logo, description, industry, website, location, contact information, and verification status. |
| **FR-05** | **Project Posting & Management** | Companies shall be able to create, edit, update, and remove freelance project listings with project descriptions, required skills, deadlines, budgets, and project categories. |
| **FR-06** | **Browse & Search Freelance Projects** | Students shall be able to browse available freelance projects and search or filter them by category, company, required skills, budget, or project type. |
| **FR-07** | **Company Profile Viewing & Trust Information** | Students shall be able to view company profiles, verification badges, company descriptions, locations, ratings, and reviews before applying for projects. |
| **FR-08** | **Project Application & Status Tracking** | Students shall be able to apply for freelance projects and monitor application statuses such as Applied, Under Review, Accepted, Rejected, or Completed. Companies shall be able to review applications and update their status. |
| **FR-09** | **Student Portfolio Viewing** | Companies shall be able to browse student profiles and portfolios to evaluate applicants before selecting suitable freelancers. |
| **FR-10** | **Messaging System** | Students and companies shall be able to exchange messages regarding freelance projects before and during collaboration. |
| **FR-11** | **Company Ratings & Reviews** | Students shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for companies after project completion to improve transparency and help future student freelancers. |
| **FR-12** | **Student Ratings & Reviews** | Companies shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for students after project completion to build students' professional reputation and credibility. |

---

## Minimum Technical Requirement

The final prototype must not be only a static presentation. It must be an interactive prototype where users can register, manage profiles, post or browse projects, apply for freelance work, communicate, and submit reviews through the main workflow.

Acceptable platforms may include:

| Platform Type | Acceptable Prototype Form |
| ------------- | ------------------------- |
| Web app | HTML/CSS/JavaScript, React.js, Spring Boot, Node.js, or similar |
| Mobile app prototype | Figma clickable prototype, Flutter, React Native, or similar |
| Database | PostgreSQL, MySQL, Firebase, or similar |
| Authentication | University email authentication for students and secure account authentication for companies using JWT |
| Data-driven prototype | Database-backed prototype containing student accounts, company accounts, company profiles, project listings, applications, messaging, and reviews |
| UI/UX prototype | Figma prototype demonstrating realistic workflows for both students and companies, from registration to project completion |
