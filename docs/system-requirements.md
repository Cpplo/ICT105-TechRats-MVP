# System Requirements

## Minimum Final Prototype Functionalities

These requirements define the minimum prototype functionalities for the Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform. The requirements are derived from the customer discovery findings, user persona, user stories, and MVP feature list. They describe the essential features required for the first functional prototype.

| Req ID | Minimum Prototype Functionality | What Students Must Show in Final Prototype |
| ------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **FR-01** | **Student Registration & Login** | Students shall be able to register and log in using their university email address to access the platform securely. |
| **FR-02** | **Student Profile Management** | Students shall be able to create, edit, and manage their profile, including personal information, skills, education, and contact details. |
| **FR-03** | **Portfolio Upload & Management** | Students shall be able to upload, edit, and manage their portfolios to showcase previous work, projects, certificates, and achievements. |
| **FR-04** | **Client Project Posting** | Companies, SMEs, and startups shall be able to create, edit, and publish freelance project listings with project descriptions, required skills, deadlines, and budgets. |
| **FR-05** | **Browse & Search Freelance Projects** | Students shall be able to browse available projects and search or filter them by category, skills, company, or project type. |
| **FR-06** | **Company Profile Viewing** | Students shall be able to view detailed company profiles, including company description, verification status, industry, location, and contact information before applying. |
| **FR-07** | **Company Ratings & Reviews** | Students shall be able to view ratings and reviews from previous student freelancers to evaluate the trustworthiness of companies. |
| **FR-08** | **Project Application & Status Tracking** | Students shall be able to submit applications for projects and monitor application statuses such as Applied, Under Review, Accepted, Rejected, or Completed. |
| **FR-09** | **Student Portfolio Viewing** | Companies shall be able to browse student profiles and portfolios before selecting suitable applicants for projects. |
| **FR-10** | **Messaging System** | Students and companies shall be able to exchange messages to discuss project details before and during collaboration. |
| **FR-11** | **Company Rating & Review Submission** | Students shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for companies after completing a project to improve platform transparency. |
| **FR-12** | **Student Rating & Review Submission** | Companies shall be able to submit ratings and reviews for students after project completion to build students' professional reputation and credibility. |

---

## Minimum Technical Requirement

The final prototype must not be only a static idea or presentation. It must be an interactive prototype where users can register, manage profiles, browse projects, apply for freelance work, communicate, and submit reviews through the main workflow.

Acceptable platforms may include:

| Platform Type | Acceptable Prototype Form |
| ------------- | ------------------------- |
| Web app | HTML/CSS/JavaScript, React.js, Spring Boot, Node.js, or similar |
| Mobile app prototype | Figma clickable prototype, Flutter, React Native, or similar |
| Database | PostgreSQL, MySQL, Firebase, or similar |
| Authentication | University email login with JWT Authentication |
| Data-driven prototype | Database-backed prototype with student profiles, company profiles, project listings, applications, messaging, and reviews |
| UI/UX prototype | Figma prototype that demonstrates realistic workflows from student registration to project completion |
